Imae did not start from a business plan. It started from remembering something I had ignored for a long time.

I grew up with my grandmother, whom I called "Imae" — Mother — in my local Isan language, because she raised me like her own child.

"Yet, I saw the pride and joy in her eyes every time she worked — whether it was weaving, sewing, or cooking."

"When I opened it, the scent of BaiNiam — her signature smell — hit my nose."
