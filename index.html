Chapter I

The Beginningจุดเริ่มต้น

Imae did not start from a business plan. It started from remembering something I had ignored for a long time.

I grew up with my grandmother, whom I called "Imae" — Mother — in my local Isan language, because she raised me like her own child. She finished only Grade 4, and spent her life working for her family without ever needing a spotlight or a stage.

"Yet, I saw the pride and joy in her eyes every time she worked — whether it was weaving, sewing, or cooking."

Her value came from within. She didn't need the world to tell her what she was worth.

That scent brought back everything. Seeing her sewing. Hearing her funny stories. Smelling the kitchen. I remembered running to her, asking for a small ball of sticky rice with salt. It wasn't fancy food, but it was the most delicious thing I had ever tasted.
