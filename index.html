Chapter I

The Beginningจุดเริ่มต้น

Imae did not start from a business plan. It started from remembering something I had ignored for a long time.

I grew up with my grandmother, whom I called "Imae" — Mother — in my local Isan language, because she raised me like her own child. She finished only Grade 4, and spent her life working for her family without ever needing a spotlight or a stage to show her talents.

"Yet, I saw the pride and joy in her eyes every time she worked — whether it was weaving, sewing, or cooking."

Her value came from within. She didn't need the world to tell her what she was worth.

The Missing Pieceสิ่งที่ขาดหาย

As a young girl, I was like any other — searching for acceptance from the outside world, following the latest trends. I ignored her stories. I even laughed at her ideas. Not realizing how important they were, until the day she passed away.

One day, while cleaning her old closet, I found a sarong she had made.

Chapter III

The Memoryความทรงจำ

That scent brought back everything. Seeing her sewing. Hearing her funny stories. Smelling the kitchen. I remembered running to her, asking for a small ball of sticky rice with salt. It wasn't fancy food, but it was the most delicious thing I had ever tasted.

"It wasn't just about the ingredients. It was because I received it in a Safe Space — a space where I didn't have to compete, or be judged by anyone."
